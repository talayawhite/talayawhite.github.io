Bread (Focaccia)

Ingredients

  • 256g Flour
  • 1c Water
  • 1Tbs Sugar
  • 1tsp Yeast
  • 1/2Tbs Salt
  • Oil
  • Toppings

Tools

  • Container for Mixing (tupperware is good)
  • Spatula
  • Container for Baking

Diections

  1. Bloom yeast in warm water with sugar.
  2. Mix together salt and flour.
  3. Mix in water with bloomed yeast.
  4. Coat dough in oil.
  5. Cover and leave somewhere cold overnight or longer.
  6. Coat baking container in oil and/or butter. Butter first if container is prone to sticking, then put in oil. If using non-stick, just oil.
  7. Transfer dough to baking container.
  8. Roll dough in oil, make sure it's coated.
  9. Leave to rise for a few hours until doubled in size.
  10. Preheat oven to 350f+. Lower for dry and more cooked, higher for less cooked.
  11. Make dimples in dough. Drizzle with more oil if needed. Add toppings.
  12. When oven is preheated, bake until crust is golden and looks yummy. 15-20 minutes at 400f.
  13. Let cool. Or don't. :)
